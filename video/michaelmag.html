Michael Matheson Maggie Centre


Maggie Centre charity cycle

Plockton 2 wheelers

Plockton Two Wheelers Maggies Charity Cycle On the 15th of August we cycled 167 miles in the day for Maggie’s Cancer Centre. We raised £17,800 which was incredible.

The Plockton Two Wheelers are: Michael Matheson, Alan Hendry, Kenneth Macrae and Douglas Cameron.

We left Duirinish at 6am from Croft Cafe and it took us 11 hours to cycle to Inverness and back. Iain Dobson and wee Annan were support drivers and Plockton legend Charlie Macrae was there to support. Welcomed home by friends and family at night, which was really special!
