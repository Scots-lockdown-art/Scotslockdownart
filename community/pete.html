Pete Garden Makeover

Pete

I am a University Biomedical Engineer and have been fortunate to be able to work from home with research and teaching.

My creativity has not been in artistic things for which I have no talent, but in construction and gardening.

These practical tasks have been a counter to my desk-based work, and allowed me, for a few hours, to lose myself to the detail of planning and execution. This has not involved computers and zoom meetings but drills, saws, rulers, hammers, wood, monoblock and lots of sand. Even better the garden looks so much nicer.
