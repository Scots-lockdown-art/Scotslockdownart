So I've started up my own social enterprise during lockdown Clifftop Projects. It is run from Dumbarton to provide artistic activities for all. So I've attached a few things I've done with the community....

1. A collection of pictures that were submitted as part of a project 'Our Westcliff' that asked our neighbourhood to draw a picture of their home. They've been done by ages 4-80! 

2. Me creating art packs to be given out to local young people to get them creative at home. 

3. Not visual art so again might not fit but still an arty creative activity....I've also been running a lot of dance classes digitally for people to get involved with from home. Some for older people, specifically those living with dementia, and younger people as well as regular adult ballet.

You can see more about those here:  https://clifftopprojects.co.uk/online-content
